      • DAY 1

      Leh (1 Night)

      Welcome to Leh airport, where you will be met and assisted by your driver who will transfer you to your hotel. You have rest of the day at leisure to acclimatize. Overnight stay in Leh.

      Dinner


      DAY 2

      Leh – Khardung La Pass – Nubra Valley (2 Nights)

      Today we proceed to Nubra Valley via Khardung La - the world’s highest motorable pass. Nubra valley - the Shyok River meets the Siachen River to form this large valley separating the Ladakh and Karakoram ranges. Upon arrival we have free time to relax and explore the surroundings. Overnight stay in Nubra.

      Breakfast Dinner


      DAY 3

      Nubra Valley

      Today we visit Diskit monastery – the oldest and largest Buddhist monastery in Nubra valley. Then we spend some time at Hunder Village and Cold desert - the only place in India where twin humped camels are found. Further we enjoy a beautiful river walk along the Shyok River. Overnight stay in Nubra.

      Breakfast Dinner


      DAY 4

      Nubra Valley – Pangong – Leh (3 Nights)

      Today we proceed to Pangong Tso, also known as Pangong Lake - one of the world's highest saltwater lakes. The most notable feature of this lake are its ever-changing hues. The lake acts as an important breeding ground for a large variety of migratory birds like Brahmani Ducks, Rare black necked cranes and Seagulls. Later we proceed to Leh via Changla pass, one of the highest motor-able road in the world. The name Changla pass literally means "Northern Pass" (Chang means north and La means Pass). Overnight stay in Leh.

      Breakfast Dinner


      DAY 5

      Leh

      Today we visit Shey Palace – used to be the summer retreat of the royal family of Ladakh, Sindhu Darshan - one of the longest rivers in Asia, Rancho School - also known as Druk White Lotus school, then we visit Hemis Gompa – a Himalayan Buddhist monastery which is believed to be the wealthiest and the biggest monastery in Leh and Photo stop at Thiksey monastery. In the evening, we have free time for shopping. Overnight stay in Leh.

      Breakfast Dinner


      DAY 6

      Leh

      Today we explore Sham Valley which includes Hall of Fame War Museum – a museum constructed by the Indian Army in memory of the brave Indian soldiers who laid down their lives defending the motherland in the Indo-Pak wars, Patthar Sahib Gurudwara - a beautiful Gurudwara sahib constructed in the memory of Guru Nanak, Magnetic Hill – also known as Gravity Hill where vehicles defy the force of gravity, Sangam of the rivers Indus & Zanskar – two of Himalaya's great rivers; Zanskar and Indus merge to form a spectacular view. Overnight stay in Leh.

      Breakfast Dinner


      DAY 7

      Leh – Airport

      Today after breakfast, proceed to Leh Airport to board your flight to your next destination or hometown. Holiday Concludes. Lets stay in touch on Facebook\email and meet again on another memorable Holiday. See you soon!
